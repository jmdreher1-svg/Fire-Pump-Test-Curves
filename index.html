Acceptance Margin (NFPA)

A single 5% downward allowance is applied to the PLD Disengaged (corrected) curve. This is based on NFPA 20's recommendation that a pump may degrade up to 5% from its rated performance, and is also used here to absorb human error in reading the gauges and the tolerance of the pressure / pitot gauges themselves (assuming all gauges are certified). A checkpoint passes if the measured value meets the rated target outright, or falls within this 5% allowance below it.
